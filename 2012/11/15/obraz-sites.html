Obraz Sites

Since the last 0.2.1 release several people have started to use Obraz for their sites. The only site with open sources that I'm aware of is labs.dendiz.com.

I keep a list of sites using Obraz. If you run your site on Obraz and you want to put a link to it on the Obraz homepage, feel free to send me an email or open a pull request.
